Shipping & Delivery

Please note that all prices on our website are excl. shipping costs. For an exact shipping quote we kindly ask you to contact us by sending us an e-mail.

We are based in the Netherlands and offer world wide insured shipping by trusted partners such as Fedex and TNT. For nearby countries we use road transport companies that are specialised in art and antiques transport.

We have experience in packing items as safe as possible to prevent damage. Ofcourse you can collect your item yourself at our storage space, this is possible by appointment only.
