About Vintz

welcoming history & artistry into your space

Marco has been on a mission with VINTZ; finding design and vintage furniture that's more than decor. Every piece we bring into our collection carries a story and a spirit, waiting to become part of your home's next chapter. We believe furniture isn't only about function; it's about meaning and awareness. That old armchair, the table marked with decades of gatherings, or the sofa shaped by the hands of a skilled craftsperson. Objects always started as an idea in one soul.

Many of our vintage pieces arrive in their original condition, which sometimes means they show signs of their journey through time. That's why we're happy to be able to offer you a reupholstery service; helping you restore and personalize your chosen piece to suit your style and home. Whenever you need guidance or want to explore options, reach out to us by email or phone, we're always here to help create your perfect piece of history.

celebrating beauty,
history and craftsmanship

We love sharing the stories behind each of our finds: the balance of form and function, the materials chosen for their beauty and durability, the passion of the makers, and the lives these objects have already touched.

And there's another reason we do what we do: choosing vintage is a conscious, eco-friendly way to furnish your home. It's about honoring the past, preserving the quality and character of well-made furniture, while making a sustainable choice for the future. We're here to help you find pieces that resonate with your own story and style.

Marco

As a collectioner, Marco combines his love for aesthetics with a talent for finding beautiful and unique pieces and stories. He enjoys seeking design treasures of great quality. His ability to connect with people and his profound knowledge of design ensures that he discovers the most refined and extraordinary furniture in Europe.
