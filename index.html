About VINTZ

Based in the Netherlands, VINTZ specializes in authentic vintage design furniture and lighting from the 1950s through the 1990s — from De Sede leather sofas and Italian mid-century masters like Mario Bellini and Tobia Scarpa to Danish design by Fabricius & Kastholm. Every piece is carefully selected for its design significance, quality, and condition. We ship worldwide, fully insured.
