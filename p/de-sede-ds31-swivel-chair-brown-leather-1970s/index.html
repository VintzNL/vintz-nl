The DS-31 swivel chair is De Sede's answer to a simple question: what does the perfect reading chair look like? High-backed, thick-cushioned, clad in dark brown leather that has broken in to a butter-soft suppleness over nearly fifty years, and mounted on an aluminium star base that lets you pivot into any position without leaving your seat.

The leather tells the story. Decades of use have produced a patina that no tannery can replicate — deeper tones in the seat where weight has pressed the hide, lighter areas on the armrests where hands have rested, and a general warmth across the entire surface that speaks of genuine, daily use. The aluminium base gleams with its original finish, and the swivel mechanism operates smoothly and silently.

This chair is in good condition with minor signs of use throughout. The structure is solid, the high back provides proper support, and the cushioning retains comfortable shape. The leather shows no cracks or tears — just the honest surface character that makes vintage De Sede so desirable. The swivel mechanism is smooth and fully functional.

De Sede designed the DS-31 range during the 1970s at their workshop in Klingnau, Switzerland. The swivel version added a practical dimension to the series — a chair that could face a fireplace one moment and a conversation the next, all without breaking the comfort of sitting. The aluminium star base was both structurally elegant and mechanically reliable, a combination that typified De Sede's engineering approach.

This is the chair for a reading corner, a home office, or any space where you want to sit properly. It rewards long hours with genuine comfort and improves any room it enters with the quiet authority of Swiss design at its most considered.
