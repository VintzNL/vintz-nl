Every collection needs a touch of humour, and this little parliament of owls delivers it with style. Five **vintage owl figurines**, gathered over the years and each made by a different pair of hands: a white **onyx** owl with wide glass eyes, a banded multicolour onyx companion, a sleek black stone owl, a hand-carved wooden one with a knowing stare, and a grey soapstone friend to complete the family.

Owl figurines were a quiet obsession of the 1960s and 70s — carved in workshops from Italy to South America, sold as symbols of wisdom and good fortune, and now hunted by collectors who appreciate their hand-made individuality.

What makes this group special is exactly that variety: five different materials, five different temperaments, one family. Lined up on a bookshelf, a windowsill or a desk, they hold a room's attention far beyond their size.

All five are in very good vintage condition, with the natural variations and small traces of age you expect from hand-worked stone and wood.
