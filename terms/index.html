FAQ & General Terms and Conditions

everything you need to know before you order

How to order?

We kindly ask you to contact us for a shipping quote by email. We process our messages every day and reply as soon as possible — due to time differences it can sometimes take a little longer. Once you confirm the total amount, we will send you an invoice.

What are the payment methods?

Payment is made by bank transfer. After you have received our invoice, you can transfer the total amount.

Do we accept returns?

Yes, we do. Once you have received your item you have a 14-day return right. This means that if you have decided the item is not as wished for, or does not fit into the space, you can send it back within 14 days of reception.

Shipping costs are for the buyer, both inbound and return costs. This also includes any customs and duties taxes.

We also charge a 15% restocking fee for cross-continental purchases — all orders outside the EU (these orders take a lot more time to prepare and have costs involved because of customs regulations and paperwork).

Should there occur damage in transit back to Vintz, this should be refunded by the buyer. Of course, we can help you organize the return shipping.

What to do in case of damage or loss?

Even with the best will in the world, it sometimes happens. Most of the items are unique and not easily replaceable, but we will always do our utmost best to solve any issues. Please contact us immediately and allow us to solve the case.

To help us handle damage claims quickly, we ask you to take pictures of the inside and outside of the box as well as the damaged item. We will then issue a claim and handle this further with the shipping company. Please allow us the needed time to file a claim and issue a refund.

Is shipping insured? And how is my order packed?

Yes, we ship insured with FedEx, DHL and Magic Movers. We always pack our items well, taking everything into account. We use bubble wrap, air cushions, extra cardboard inside the box and so on. For larger and heavier items we use a company that crates the items for us.

How fast can I receive my item(s)?

Depending on how fast you need your items and where you are located, we can offer an express service. Before buying an item you can contact us to get an exact quote. We always provide you with tracking information and estimated delivery times.

I am a trader, interior designer, ...

Yes! We love to be part of your project. We have provided lamps and pieces of furniture for different projects, especially in the USA. We can offer trade prices and arrange bulk shipments to make it all the more attractive.

We know there are a lot of specialized dealers out there and are happy to supply you with interesting prices for specific pieces. Feel free to contact us! Remember that we hunt down lots of flea markets, auctions and more, so we can always look out for a specific item or items.

Electricity & safety standards

All our lamps have been rewired in compliance with EU safety standards. This does not automatically mean that these lights comply with the standards in your country.

Our lamps work around the globe with different voltages, as long as you use light bulbs that work in your country. We deliver our lamps with plugs, but these might be different depending on your location.

Import duties & taxes

All our items ship from the Netherlands (EU). We ship duty free to every country in the EU. For intercontinental shipping it happens that duties are applied. For vintage items they usually won't be that high, and most of the time it is exempt.

It is impossible to check the duty rules for every country depending on the object. Therefore we state that the buyer is responsible for any additional duty or tax payment. You are always free to contact us if there is a doubtfully high amount to pay.

Privacy

This Privacy Policy describes how and when we collect, use, and share information when you purchase an item from us, contact us, or otherwise use our services or its related sites and services.

Information we collect

To fulfill your order, you must provide us with certain information, such as your name, email address, postal address, payment information, and the details of the product that you're ordering. You may also choose to provide us with additional personal information (for a custom order, for example) if you contact us directly.

Why we need your information and how we use it

Information sharing and disclosure

Information about our customers is important to our business. We share your personal information for very limited reasons and in limited circumstances, as follows: we engage certain trusted third parties to perform functions and provide services to our shop, such as delivery companies. We will share your personal information with these third parties, but only to the extent necessary to perform these services.

Compliance with laws. We may collect, use, retain, and share your information if we have a good-faith belief that it is reasonably necessary to (a) respond to legal process or to government requests; (b) enforce our agreements, terms and policies; (c) prevent, investigate, and address fraud and other illegal activity, security, or technical issues; or (d) protect the rights, property, and safety of our customers, or others.

Data retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide you with our services and as described in this Privacy Policy. However, we may also be required to retain this information to comply with our legal and regulatory obligations, to resolve disputes, and to enforce our agreements. We generally keep your data for the following time period: 5 years.

Transfers of personal information outside the EU

We may store and process your information through third-party hosting services in the US and other jurisdictions. As a result, we may transfer your personal information to a jurisdiction with different data protection and government surveillance laws than your jurisdiction.

Your rights

If you reside in certain territories, including the EU, you have a number of rights in relation to your personal information. While some of these rights apply generally, certain rights apply only in certain limited cases:

Access. You may have the right to access and receive a copy of the personal information we hold about you by contacting us using the contact information below.

Change, restrict, delete. You may also have rights to change, restrict our use of, or delete your personal information. Absent exceptional circumstances (such as where we are required to store data for legal reasons) we will generally delete your personal information upon request.

Object. You can object to (i) our processing of some of your information based on our legitimate interests, and (ii) receiving marketing messages from us after providing your express consent to receive them. In such cases, we will delete your personal information unless we have compelling and legitimate grounds to continue using that information or if it is needed for legal reasons.

Complain. If you reside in the EU and wish to raise a concern about our use of your information (and without prejudice to any other rights you may have), you have the right to do so with your local data protection authority.

How to contact us

For purposes of EU data protection law, we, Vintz, are the data controller of your personal information. If you have any questions or concerns, you may contact us at info@vintz.nl.
